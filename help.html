Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I get started after purchase?

After purchase, you'll receive an email with access to our onboarding form within 15 minutes. Complete this form with your business preferences, and we'll begin setting up your custom business package. Setup typically takes 24-72 hours.

What information do I need for onboarding?

You'll need to provide your business niche preference, desired domain name (we'll help if needed), branding preferences (colors, style), and contact information. The form takes about 10-15 minutes to complete.

Do I need technical skills?

No technical skills required! We handle all the technical setup for you. Your business comes ready-to-run with full training materials. Most customers are generating revenue within the first week.

Refunds & Policies

What is your refund policy?

Full refunds are available ONLY before you submit the onboarding form. Once you submit onboarding information, we begin custom work and no refunds are possible. Please review our detailed refund policy before purchasing.

Why can't I get a refund after onboarding?

Once you submit the onboarding form, we immediately begin creating your custom business package. This includes purchasing domains, licenses, and creating personalized content that cannot be resold to other customers.

How do I request a refund?

Email refunds@businessbuiltfast.com with your order number and "Refund Request - Pre-Onboarding" in the subject line. Refunds are only processed if you haven't submitted the onboarding form yet.

Support & Training

What support do I get?

You receive 90 days of email support, access to our private community, complete video training library, and weekly group coaching calls (depending on your package). We respond to support requests within 24-48 hours.

How quickly will you respond to support requests?

We aim to respond to all support requests within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). Urgent technical issues are prioritized and typically receive same-day responses.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we provide support primarily through email and our community forum. This allows us to provide detailed, documented responses and maintain our cost-effective pricing.

Business & Revenue

How quickly can I start making money?

Most customers see their first revenue within 1-2 weeks of receiving their business. However, building to $1K+/month typically takes 30-90 days depending on your effort and business type.

Are the income claims realistic?

The examples shown are from real customers, but individual results vary significantly based on effort, market conditions, and execution. There are no income guarantees - success depends on your commitment and business acumen.

Can I sell my business later?

Yes! You own your business 100% and can sell it on platforms like Flippa, Empire Flippers, or privately. Many customers have successfully sold their businesses for 10-20x monthly revenue.
