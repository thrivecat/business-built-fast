Due to the bespoke and customized nature of our services, our refund policy has specific conditions that you must understand and accept before making a purchase.

1. Refund Eligibility

1.1 Full Refund Available

You are eligible for a FULL REFUND (100% of your purchase price) if ALL of the following conditions are met:

  • You request the refund BEFORE submitting the onboarding form
  • The refund request is made within 30 days of purchase
  • You have not accessed or downloaded any custom materials prepared for you
  • You have not received any personalized consultation or setup services

1.2 No Refunds After Onboarding

CRITICAL: Once you submit the onboarding form, you are NOT eligible for any refund. This policy exists because:

  • We immediately begin creating your customized business package based on your specifications
  • Resources are allocated and work begins on your bespoke setup
  • Custom domains, hosting, and software licenses may be purchased on your behalf
  • Personalized content and configurations are created specifically for you

2. The Onboarding Process

After your purchase, you will receive an email with access to our onboarding form. This form collects essential information including:

  • Your business preferences and niche selection
  • Customization requirements
  • Branding preferences
  • Technical specifications
  • Contact and delivery information

WARNING: By submitting this form, you acknowledge that you are waiving your right to a refund and authorizing us to begin work on your custom business package.

3. Why This Policy Exists

Our strict refund policy after onboarding submission is necessary because:

  • Immediate Resource Allocation: We assign team members to your project immediately
  • Third-Party Costs: We may purchase domains, hosting, and licenses on your behalf
  • Custom Work: All work is personalized and cannot be resold to another customer
  • Intellectual Property: Custom strategies and content are created specifically for your business
  • Time Investment: Our team invests significant time in researching and building your specific solution

4. How to Request a Refund

If you are eligible for a refund (have not submitted the onboarding form), follow these steps:

  1. Email our support team at refunds@businessbuiltfast.com
  2. Include your order number and purchase date
  3. State "Refund Request - Pre-Onboarding" in the subject line
  4. Provide the reason for your refund request (optional but helpful)

Refund requests are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Funds will be returned to your original payment method within 5-10 business days depending on your financial institution.

5. Exceptions and Special Circumstances

We understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. While our policy is strict, we will consider exceptions ONLY in the following cases:

  • Technical Failure: We are unable to deliver the promised service due to our technical issues
  • Misrepresentation: The service delivered is fundamentally different from what was advertised
  • Non-Delivery: We fail to deliver your package within 30 days of onboarding submission

These exceptions are evaluated on a case-by-case basis and require documentation.

6. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds are NOT available. Our packages are comprehensive solutions, and individual components cannot be separated or refunded independently.

7. Chargebacks and Disputes

Before initiating a chargeback with your financial institution, please contact us first. Chargebacks harm both parties and may result in:

  • Immediate termination of all services
  • Removal of access to all materials and communities
  • Potential legal action to recover costs
  • Reporting to credit agencies if applicable

8. Quality Guarantee

While we cannot offer refunds after onboarding, we DO guarantee:

  • Delivery of all promised components in your package
  • 90 days of support to ensure proper setup
  • Access to our community for ongoing assistance
  • Resolution of any technical issues with the delivered package

9. Your Acknowledgment

By making a purchase from Business Built Fast, you acknowledge that:

  • You have read and understood this refund policy
  • You understand that submitting the onboarding form waives your right to a refund
  • You accept the bespoke nature of our services
